## Style Prompt

Luxury Athens penthouse cinematic background: dark architectural atmosphere, Acropolis glow, warm metal linework, glass reflections, marble texture, slow editorial motion. The composition is designed to sit behind bilingual website copy, so it contains no visible words, captions, labels, numerals, or language-specific UI text.

## Colors

- Obsidian night: `#070707`
- Acropolis gold: `#C9A84C`
- Warm marble: `#F5F0E8`
- Smoked glass: `rgba(255,255,255,0.10)`
- Deep bronze: `#5A4324`

## Typography

No visible typography inside the composition. Website text is layered separately by the app.

## What NOT to Do

- Do not add readable text, letters, numerals, captions, or translated copy.
- Do not use generic blue/purple gradients.
- Do not cover the Acropolis view with heavy cards.
- Do not use jumpy motion; keep movement slow, architectural, and premium.
